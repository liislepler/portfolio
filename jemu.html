JEMU Solutions

Branding / Graphic Design / Social Media

Figma / Illustrator / Photoshop

In March 2024, I started working together with a consulting company, JEMU Solutions, based in Gothenburg, Sweden. My task included redesigning the logo, creating new designs for branding such as logos for the different branches that they specialize in as well as creating the content such as taking photos of the employees. As of now, the branding primarily extends to their LinkedIn profile and presentations for potential clients, yet they are a growing company.

It has been a very fun and eye-opening journey working together with so many professionals and learning about their experiences as well as bonding with their lovely besökshund Jack!

Currently, I am also working together with JEMU Solutions’ client UNI5, which is a car racing organization, and I am redesigning their logo and creating other branding materials for promotion.

Client's wishes

  • Enhance their visual identity to accommodate their expanding team, including their newest member, Jack, the family dog, who is currently undergoing training to become a visiting dog.
  • Create a cohesive and engaging brand image that reflects the diverse expertise across their specialized branches while maintaining a professional appearance.
  • Establish visual connections between their various branches to reinforce brand cohesion and identity.
  • Revamp their current logo to align with the refreshed brand identity.

Banner

The banner showcases logos representing the specialized branches of the company's members: services, economics, business, and healthcare.

Logo

The logo combines the initials of the founding members, blending them together to create a unified symbol. Each letter represents a different person, but together they show unity and collaboration.

Linkedin

On Linkedin, you can find posts introducing each team member, highlighting their specialties, expertise, and educational backgrounds.
