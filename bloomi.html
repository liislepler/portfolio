Bloomi

UI/UX Design

Figma

Bloomi is a personal project inspired by my observation of many flower shop websites' UI/UX designs. I noticed a lack of options for personalization, particularly when ordering custom bouquets online. This inspired the concept of Bloomi, a flower shop that allows users to play around and create their own flower bouquet by selecting colors, flowers, greenery, and even packaging.

The website has a user-friendly interface, providing easy navigation with a step-by-step timeline at the bottom of the page. The entire project was designed on Figma, and I thoroughly enjoyed the process of designing each step to ensure customers can effortlessly create the most unique flower bouquet according to their preferences.
