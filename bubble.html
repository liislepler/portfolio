Bubble Garden

Branding / Graphic Design / Package Design

Photoshop / Illustrator / Figma

Bubble Garden is a personal project I completed in 4 days, focused on exploring packaging design. Initially, I developed a moodboard, a simple branding guide, and designed the logo for this handmade soap company. Drawing inspiration from my background in illustration—a hobby I've enjoyed for many years—I challenged myself with digital vector illustration, creating the packaging designs using Adobe Illustrator and afterwards designing the full package on Adobe Photoshop. Additionally, I tried out debossing methods for the soap and paid special attention to typography for the label design.

This project was an enjoyable experience that deepened my passion for graphic design, pattern creation, and experimenting with colors and shapes.
