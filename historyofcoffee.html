The History of Coffee

Animation / Video Editing / Sound Editing / Graphic Design

After Effects / Photoshop / Illustrator / Figma

I was part of an exciting animation project in which we delved into the captivating history of coffee, emphasizing the amusing and funny tales surrounding the journey of a coffee and how it became one of the world's most cherished beverages. The animation was created using After Effects and Photoshop, allowing us to bring the engaging narrative to life.

Co-creators: Nicki Mirani, Elin Tri, Thishaya Ukwatta
