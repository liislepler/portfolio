Branding / Graphic Design / UI/UX Design / Social Media

InDesign / Photoshop / Illustrator / Figma / After Effects

As part of the Graphic Design for New Media course, my team and I collaborated on a project to create a branding campaign for an art event. Our tasks ranged from crafting an animated logo to creating a graphical profile with detailed guidelines on the use of colors and typefaces. Additionally, we designed social media posts, a website, and an app interface to complement the branding strategy. Throughout the project, we used a combination of design tools, including InDesign, Photoshop, After Effects, and Figma, to bring our creative vision to life.

Co-creators: Elin Tri, Sofie Kastelli, Nicki Mirani, Thishaya Ukwatta

I actively contributed to the decision-making and design processes within our group. With effective communication and collaborative brainstorming, we collectively shaped the project's direction. My primary areas of focus included designing the website and creating engaging social media posts. Through teamwork and shared creativity, our group successfully brought our ideas into reality.
