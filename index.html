UI/UX Designer

World Simplified

My internship at World Simplified as a UI/UX Designer was an incredibly enriching experience. When I joined the startup, it was in its early stages, still finding its visual identity. I had the opportunity to contribute to revamping their graphical profile, including adjusting the color scheme and aligning the logo with the new design.

Collaborating with fellow UI/UX designers and web developers, we dedicated long hours on Figma to design the entire website platform for the service. Through this process, I discovered and fell in love with Figma and many of its features.

While challenging, the experience of collaborating with people from around the world was fascinating. Working remotely taught me valuable lessons in time management and workload prioritization. I developed my communication skills, learning to effectively convey my vision to team members, whether they were developers or business professionals.
